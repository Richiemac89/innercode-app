# Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Get Up and Running in 3 Steps

### 1. Install Dependencies
```bash
cd innercode-app
npm install
```

### 2. Start the Development Server
```bash
npm run dev
```

### 3. Open in Browser
The app will automatically open at `http://localhost:3000`

---

## 🎯 First Time Using the App?

1. **Click "Reveal the real you"** on the home screen
2. **Read the instructions** to understand how the app works
3. **Answer the questions** honestly across 12 life categories
4. **Rate each category** from 0-10 based on how you're doing
5. **View your results** - your values, personal code, and suggestions
6. **Start journaling** to refine your profile over time

---

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific** in your answers - stories and examples help
- **Write longer responses** when you can (30+ words)
- **Journal regularly** - the app learns from your entries
- **Tag your journal entries** with categories and values
- **Review your results** after 2 weeks of journaling

---

## 📱 Using the App

### Navigation
- Use the **burger menu** (top right) to navigate between pages
- All your data is saved automatically in your browser

### Journaling
- One entry per day allowed
- Tag with categories and values for better insights
- View past entries in the calendar view

### Privacy
- Everything stays on your device
- No data leaves your browser
- Clear browser data to reset
